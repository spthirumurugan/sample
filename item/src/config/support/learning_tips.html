Planning and Carrying Out Investigations
As part of investigations, scientists make observations and collect and record data as evidence. They use this evidence to explain the world around them.
You will make observations and collect and record data about motion. Graphing the data will help you see patterns in motion.
Patterns
Scientists look for patterns in their data. They use patterns to predict what will happen and to support claims.
You will look for patterns in the motion of carnival games.
You can use the patterns of motion to make predictions about the games.
