Think about how you can describe the motion in each game. What do you do to make the motion change?

Pendulum Prize: What pattern of motion do you expect? How can you make the ball at the end of the pendulum reach each prize?

Ring the Bell: Can you ring the bell? What can you do to make the bell ringer go higher?

Shuffleboard: How can you change the force to make the puck land in each circle? Every time you push the puck, you can change the strength of the force or the direction.
